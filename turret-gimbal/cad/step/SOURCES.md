# STEP model sources for the assembly

Drop downloaded STEP/STP files into this folder (`turret-gimbal/cad/step/`).
The plate solids generated by `tools/freecad_build.py` land in `step/plates/`.

> I could not auto-download these: McMaster, NVIDIA (Jetson), and StepperOnline
> gate CAD behind login / interactive sessions / anti-bot, so they need a human
> with a browser. Links below take you straight to each part's CAD download.

## Tip
For screws/standoffs, the **FreeCAD Fasteners workbench** generates ISO/DIN
hardware on the fly — faster than sourcing a STEP per fastener.
